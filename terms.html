Terms and Conditions for Retreats

Payment Policy

To reserve your spot on a Dugong Surf Retreat, we require a non-refundable deposit. The exact amount depends on which retreat you're joining - we'll send you all the details after you book. This deposit holds your space and confirms you agree to our terms.

The full balance is due 30 days before your retreat starts. Like the deposit, this final payment is non-refundable. If we don't receive payment by the deadline, we may need to cancel your booking and won't be able to return any payments you've already made.

We accept bank transfers (usually fee-free) and credit cards (5% processing fee applies). Please double-check our payment details with us before sending money.

If You Need to Cancel

We get it - life happens! But because we commit to venues and instructors in advance, we can't offer refunds for cancellations made more than 30 days before your retreat. If you cancel:

  • We'll try to fill your spot - if we can, you may transfer 50% to a future retreat
  • We can't refund flights, travel delays, or illness (this is why travel insurance is so important)

If We Need to Cancel

While rare, we might cancel if we don't get enough participants. In this case, we'll refund everything you've paid (including your deposit). For cancellations due to weather or events beyond our control, refunds depend on what we can recover from our partners. We always recommend buying travel insurance to cover flights and other expenses.

The Unpredictable Stuff

"Force majeure" means things we couldn't prevent - natural disasters, political issues, pandemics, etc. If these affect your retreat, we'll do our best to help but can't promise refunds.

Travel Insurance

Seriously - get it! Good coverage should include:

  • Trip cancellation
  • Medical emergencies
  • Lost luggage
  • Coverage if we have to cancel

Important Legal Bits

By joining us, you agree that:

  • You're physically able to participate in surf/yoga activities
  • You'll let us know about any health conditions
  • We're not responsible for injuries, travel issues, or lost belongings
  • We may use photos from the retreat (we'll make you look good!)
  • We reserve the right to refuse participants or adjust itineraries for safety

When you book with Dugong Surf, these terms become our agreement. We're stoked to have you join the tribe!
